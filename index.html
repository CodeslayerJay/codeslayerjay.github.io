Education Summary:

Openclassrooms Bachelors Degree program is a unique program. It is self-paced program that only teaches the skills for performing tasks required of a developer. The course work is based on real world scenarios and projects. Students gain experience like they are working as a developer by solving real world projects. Each student is assigned an industry professional as a mentor who guides and bestows their industy expertise. Every project must be presented to an assessor who then validates the students understanding and that the requirements are met for each project. These are pass or fail assessments. This ensures that each student demonstrates their ability to provide the value employers are looking for.
